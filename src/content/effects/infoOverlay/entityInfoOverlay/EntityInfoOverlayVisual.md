──────────────────────────────
USAGE & EXTENSIBILITY
──────────────────────────────
- Can be extended to show:
  - Health, energy, shield, or resource bars
  - Ownership, team, or faction indicators
  - Targeting, selection, or focus highlights
  - Event triggers, collision feedback, or effect overlays
  - Relationships: links, groups, parent/child, orbit paths
  - Tutorial overlays, onboarding steps, contextual help
  - Cosmic lore popups, story snippets, or entity history
  - Spectator mode overlays, player stats, or live commentary
  - Achievement, quest, or milestone progress
  - Audio-reactive visuals, music sync, or rhythm feedback
  - AR/VR overlays for immersive modes
  - Accessibility overlays (screen reader, keyboard hints)
  - Export/share overlays (scene info, entity metadata)
  - Debug overlays for advanced diagnostics, dev tools
- Style and content are fully customizable for each game or universe.
- Designed for both developer diagnostics and player-facing overlays.
